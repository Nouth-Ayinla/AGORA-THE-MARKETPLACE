Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers.

Do I need a CAC or business registration to sell? expand_more

No! Just your student ID. That's it. We designed Agora for students, not businesses. Upload your student ID and you're ready to start selling in minutes.

How fast do I get paid? expand_more

Within 3 days. No waiting 30 days. Get a payment every 3 days straight to your bank account.

What if I have exams? Can I pause my shop? expand_more

Yes, Exam Mode is your safety net. Toggle it on and your shop gets paused. No new orders, no stress. Resume whenever you're ready. Your education comes first.

Can I sell both products and services? expand_more

Yes! One of our key features. Sell your baked goods on Monday, offer graphics design services on Tuesday — all on one Agora profile.

What's the commission in the Founding Member plan? expand_more

0% first month, 5% second month, then 8% forever. Limited to the first 1,000 members. After that, it's 10% for everyone else.

How do I get help if something goes wrong? expand_more

Real humans, 24/7. Chat, call, or WhatsApp us. Not a bot. Not waiting 48 hours. We answer.
